About this space

Escape to our charming alpine cottage nestled in the heart of Zermatt, offering breathtaking views of the iconic Matterhorn. This cozy 2-bedroom retreat features rustic wooden beams, a stone fireplace, and modern amenities for the perfect Swiss mountain getaway. Wake up to panoramic mountain vistas from your private balcony, then explore world-class skiing just minutes from your door. The cottage includes a fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops, comfortable living area with plush seating, and two well-appointed bedrooms with premium linens. Located in a quiet residential area, you'll enjoy peaceful surroundings while being just a 10-minute walk from Zermatt's vibrant village center with its shops, restaurants, and the famous Gornergrat railway station.
